# Code of Conduct

AFM Studio is meant to be a welcoming project for everyone. We want people of any background or experience level to feel comfortable taking part.

## The basics

- Be kind and respectful.
- Assume good intent and give people the benefit of the doubt.
- Welcome questions, including beginner questions.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Keep it inclusive. Harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks are not allowed.

## Not okay

- Insults, slurs, or demeaning comments.
- Harassment in any form, public or private.
- Sharing someone else's private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions, issues, or pull requests.

## Reporting

If you see behavior that breaks these rules, or you are on the receiving end of it, contact the maintainers at taylor@techopolis.app or mikedoise@icloud.com. Reports are handled privately.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, commits, issues, and pull requests that do not follow this code of conduct, and may block repeat or serious offenders from the project.

This code of conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and any other AFM Studio community channels.
